Details

Please join and help me celebrate my 40th birthday, 1920’s style!

The exact location is a secret, but a meeting location and detailed instructions will be telegraphed 24 hours before the party — be prepared to travel to a discreet location in San Francisco's North Beach neighborhood.

We’ll begin promptly at 6:00PM with a private reception for birthday party attendees to meet one another, listen to a six piece jazz band, and learn how to make prohibition-era cocktails.

At 8:00PM we’ll join with other audience members for an immersive theatrical experience: you’ll be free to explore an expansive nightclub, sip on handcrafted cocktails, test your luck in the casino, enjoy exciting performances, and interact with the many characters as they go about their evening.

A dress code is strictly enforced & emdash; 1920’s apparel is preferred, cocktail attire is accepted. There will be a professional photographer taking cadids at the private reception, so come dressed to impress!

Tickets will be purchased on your behalf for the immersive theater portion of the evening. No tickets are required for the private reception, but space is limited. RSVP by March 31st to secure your spot for either or both events.
